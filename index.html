About Us

Founded in 2000 by Mr. Gobi, RS Cottage Industries specializes in processing, manufacturing, and producing high-quality, natural sun-dried vathals. Our vision is to deliver authentic and chemical-free traditional products, preserving the rich culinary heritage of India. We aim to expand our global reach while adhering to quality and sustainability standards.
